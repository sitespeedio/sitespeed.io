## Contribute
* * *

We'd love your help making sitespeed.io better — code, docs, design, bug reports, or just letting us know what's missing. The [help section](https://github.com/sitespeedio/sitespeed.io/blob/main/HELP.md) is a good place to start, and the [issue list](https://github.com/sitespeedio/sitespeed.io/issues) shows what's on our plate. Thanks to [everyone who has already contributed](https://github.com/sitespeedio/sitespeed.io/blob/main/CONTRIBUTORS.md) — it really does make a difference.
